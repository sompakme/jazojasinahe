13 Best Designer Backpacks of 2024

The Le Pliage backpack is a forever travel bag for a reason. Made from Longchamp's signature nylon material, this backpack will take you on all of life's adventures in style. Featuring long, adjustable leather straps, you'll be able to customize the length for optimal comfort depending on your size. A fold over the top reveals roomy pockets inside to stow your necessities.

Dimensions: 11"H x 10"W x 3 3/4"D

Colors: Navy, Black, Red, Pink, Green

What reviewers are saying: "This is the perfect size for travel. It folds up to envelope size in the suitcase, small enough that you do not have to check it in the museums, holds as much as a purse, is lightweight and distributes weight evenly so one shoulder isn't carrying the weight, pick pocket proof, and you can toss it into the washing machine. Best of all it is highly water resistant and will keep your things dry in a downpour."
