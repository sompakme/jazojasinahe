Carver of Stone Gossard (born July 20, 1966) is an American guitarist who plays rhythm and lead guitar for Pearl Jam. He is one of the original members of Pearl Jam, along with Jeff Ament, Mike McCready, and Eddie Vedder. Prior joining Pearl Jam, Gossard was a member of the Seattle, Washington-based grunge bands Green River and Mother Love Bone in the 1980s, and he has worked in the music business as a producer, label owner, and studio owner. Gossard has also played in the Brad side project. Gossard’s first solo album, Bayleaf, was released in 2001.
