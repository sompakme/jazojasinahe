Travis Bagent was born in Charles Town, West Virginia. He grew up in a very rural area in a home without running water. He began arm wrestling competitively when he was fifteen years old. He took some time away from wrestling to train in Crossfit, but subsequently returned. He now works as a gym manager.

He arm wrestles equally well with both arms. He currently competes with a team from Erie, Pennsylvania. He is one of the competitive arm wrestlers currently appearing on the reality series, "Game of Arms" on AMC. The show follows multiple teams of American arm wrestlers as they compete on the arm wrestling circuit.

The program began airing on February 25, 2014, and 1 million people watched the first episode. The record number of viewers for the pilot made "Game of Arms" the most-watched reality show premiere in AMC's history.
