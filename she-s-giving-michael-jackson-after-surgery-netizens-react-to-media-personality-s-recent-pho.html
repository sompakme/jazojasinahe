Michael Jackson's moonwalk hat auctioned

In more entertainment news, Legit.ng reported that Michael Jackson's moonwalk hat was put up for sale at the Hotel Drouot in Paris.

Organiser Arthur Perault of the Artpeges gallery admitted that valuations for Jackson items had fallen lately due to "the sale of fakes and the accusations against him".
