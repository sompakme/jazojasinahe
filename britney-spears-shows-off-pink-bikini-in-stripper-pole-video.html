Britney Spears shows off pink bikini in stripper pole video

After getting over her recent divorce, Britney Spears is continuing to live her best life, or at the very least put out videos which suggest as much.

Uploading sexy videos to her Instagram is nothing new for Spears, however she left her fans stunned with a video which showed her working a stripper pole whilst wearing a pink bikini.

Having always had a stunning figure, Spears has shown no real signs of ageing, and her moves on the pole were flabbergasting for those who viewed the video.

What song did Spears dance to?

The video of Britney on the pole was set to music, specifically 'Don't Hurt Yourself' by Beyonce, and she also had a gold-chain trim on the bikini.

Britney swayed her long blonde hair during the dance, whilst she also wore a black choker around her neck alongside black platform heels.

The stripper pole was pink and it matched the bikini, however this wasn't the first time the singer has posted herself using the pole.

Previous entries have seen Spears dancing on the pole in a leopard print bikini, which also caught the eye.

Spears' freedom on social media came about when her conservatorship came to an end in November 2021, and she has looked to live life to the fullest ever since then.

However, such liberation has brought a degree of pain, with her marriage ending in a difficult divorce amidst rumors that the man in question was even hired by her father to keep tabs on Britney.

Whilst that hasn't yet been confirmed in terms of evidence, it shows just how difficult it was for Britney to find some freedom during her more difficult years, and she is now showcasing that she can do whatever she wants, whenever she wants.
