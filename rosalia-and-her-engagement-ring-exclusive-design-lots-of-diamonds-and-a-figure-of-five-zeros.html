Rosalia and her engagement ring: exclusive design, lots of diamonds... and a figure of five zeros!

It has undoubtedly been the most talked about news in the world of music, the tabloids and the entertainment world in recent days: The couple formed by Rosalia and Rauw Alejandro has said 'I do' and the image of the Catalan crying has gone around the world.

The Puerto Rican and the Spaniard began dating in 2020 and have chosen a song made between them to let the world and all their followers know that they have married.

In total, there are three songs that the couple have created, 'Beso', 'Vampiros' and 'Promesa'. The end of the video clip of 'Beso', released at midnight last night, went around the world because of the image it contained in the form of news. Rosalia, crying "with mascara running", held in her hand an engagement ring box which she was already wearing on her finger.

This is Rosalia's ring: It's worth 600,000 euros!

A spectacular ring with a classic cut, the opposite of the singer's current style. It is a one-piece ring made of white gold and a large diamond in the center. This main stone is surrounded by others of smaller size forming a triangle.

The figure has already been released on Saturday. And it takes away the hiccups. Because the value of the engagement ring that Rosalia is going to wear is 600,000 euros. A real luxury...

The clue that Rosalia left about her ring

For the most absent-minded, a detail may have been overlooked... Rosalia already wore this ring last year! Specifically, in June, where Rosalia uploaded a photo to Instagram embracing Rauw Alejandro, who had his back turned. On the artist's hand, you can see the same diamond ring.

The other more mediatic rings

Within the music industry we have to talk about the most famous marriages and the most expensive rings. The list is led, of course, by Beyonce and Jay-Z. The rapper gave her a ring from the jeweler Lorraine Schwartz, composed of an 18-carat emerald diamond and other smaller ones. It cost five million euros.

Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck got married and the singer wore a Harry Winston ring, whose price was around 1.2 million dollars. Kate Middleton, meanwhile, wore a 500,000 dollar ring, a gift from her husband Prince William.
