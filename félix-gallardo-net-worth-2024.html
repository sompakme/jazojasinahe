Who is Félix Gallardo?

Miguel Ángel Félix Gallardo, commonly known as Félix Gallardo, is a notorious figure in the world of drug trafficking.

He was the founder of the Guadalajara Cartel in the 1980s and was instrumental in the establishment of the modern Mexican drug trade.

Despite his criminal activities, there is a significant interest in his net worth, especially considering his influence and power during his prime.

This article aims to provide an in-depth analysis of Félix Gallardo’s estimated net worth in 2024.

Early Life and Rise to Power

Félix Gallardo was born in a rural community in the Mexican state of Sinaloa. He started his career as a police officer before turning to drug trafficking.

His rise to power was swift, and by the 1980s, he was one of the most powerful drug lords in Mexico.

Establishment of the Guadalajara Cartel

Félix Gallardo founded the Guadalajara Cartel in the late 1970s.

The cartel quickly gained control of most of the drug trafficking in Mexico and the corridors along the U.S.-Mexico border.

The cartel’s operations were highly profitable, contributing significantly to Félix Gallardo’s wealth.

Peak of Power and Wealth

At the height of his power in the mid-1980s, Félix Gallardo was reportedly worth around $500 million.

His wealth came from the vast drug trafficking operations he controlled, which included the production, transportation, and sale of marijuana and cocaine.

Downfall and Arrest

Félix Gallardo’s downfall began in the late 1980s when he was implicated in the murder of a U.S. Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) agent.

He was arrested in 1989 and has been in prison since then. His arrest led to the fragmentation of the Guadalajara Cartel into several smaller cartels.

Current Net Worth
