Adam Cole Undergoes Ankle Surgery

Adam Cole suffered an ankle injury at AEW Dynamite Grand Slam, and he wasn’t even in a match at the time. Cole was just walking to the ring, and he landed badly on his foot when he hopped off the ramp. Now, he is facing a long road to recovery.

Many fans wondered when Adam Cole would get his surgery. After all, he appeared on AEW television for few weeks with a walking boot on, but he didn’t have his surgery. Now, that has changed.

Kevin Owens was the first to break the news that Adam Cole had surgery on Friday. Now, we have confirmation of that.

Adam Cole said that he will need multiple surgeries on his ankle after that devastating injury. He appeared in some video vignettes over the past couple of weeks, each time saying that he needed to go have surgery. Now, we have to wonder when he will show up again.

MJF is now preoccupied with Bullet Club gold, and that controversial storyline is getting some attention. Hopefully, MJF will be waiting for his best friend Cole whenever he can return.
