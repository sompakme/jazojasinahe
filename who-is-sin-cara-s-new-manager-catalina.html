Who Is Sin Caras New Manager Catalina?

WWE surprised everyone when they debuted a masked female manager of accompany Sin Cara to the ring this week, but who is Carolina?

Click here for our complete WWE RAW results.

NXT recently signed Catalina Garcia to a contract, so she is officially with the company. It’s unclear if her partnership with Sin Cara will continue, but they definitely seemed to start something. She still got a chance to impress with her ability.

Garcia wrestled for various lucha promotions for the past five years before signing with WWE. The Chile native could get a great opportunity on the main roster out of nowhere.

It should be noted that WWE spelled Catalina’s name as “Carolina” during their entrance.
