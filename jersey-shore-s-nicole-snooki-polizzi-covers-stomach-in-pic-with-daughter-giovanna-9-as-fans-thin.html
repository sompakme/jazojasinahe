Jersey Shore's Nicole 'Snooki' Polizzi covers stomach in pic with daughter Giovanna, 9, as fans thin

JERSEY Shore fans have noticed something strange in Nicole (Snooki) Polizzi's photo with her daughter.

Fans speculated that the reality TV star could be pregnant with her 4th child after she seemed to strategically hide her stomach in her Instagram post.

Snookie, 35, posted a photo with her daughter on Sunday.

She put her arms around her daughter, Giovanna, 9, who was dressed in a cheerleading uniform with a varsity jacket on top.

Snooki wore a back jacket over a black top and stood just behind her daughter as she smiled at the camera.

She finished the look with a black baseball cap with her daughter's cheer logo on the front.
