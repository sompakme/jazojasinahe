Lil Tracy Trashes Juice WRLD After Dropping Lil Peep Tribute Track

There seems to be an unusual situation building between two young rappers that have shown a lot of promise. Lil Tracy arguably made his best music with Lil Peep and, when Peep tragically passed away last year, Tracy mourned for a long time. As he returns to form, there may be some jealousy forming for the stars just beginning to flourish. A nobody in most people's eyes a few years ago, Juice WRLD has begun his world takeover, becoming one of the names to watch out for in hip-hop. Moving in a comparable territory as the late Lil Peep and XXXTentacion in terms of their connections to emo-rap, Juice released a tribute song titled "Legends" this week, honoring the two fallen soldiers. Tracy appears to have taken issue with the song and its creator, going after Juice on Twitter in a strange feud.

While the two appear to be patching things up already, Tracy let off some steam earlier today, addressing a harsh message to the Chicago rapper on Twitter. "Yeah I said it fuck juice world," wrote Tracy, continuing to interact with fans who were shocked at his message. Including a middle finger emoji and revealing that he likes the rising star's music but is ultimately "jealous of his success," Tracy seemed to switch gears to prevent any confusion.
