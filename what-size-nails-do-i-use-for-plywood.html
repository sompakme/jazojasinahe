What size nails do I use for plywood?

Answer

Nails with a diameter of 6d – 8d (finishing) are excellent for 1/2′′ thick plywood. If the plywood is 3/8′′ thick, the finishing nails in the 4d – 6d size are appropriate. 3/4′′ – 1′′ is the most appropriate thickness for 1/4′′ thick plywood. Brads should be used for the 3/4′′ – 1′′ nail type.
