What is the movie in too deep about?

Answer

Following the path of a recent cadet graduate from the police school, Jeffrey Cole (Omar Epps), this film chronicles his goals of going undercover to assist in the capture and prosecution of high-profile professional criminals. For a few missions busting low-to-mid-level crooks, he is given the opportunity to go after the big fish, Dwayne Gittens, AKA God, and his crew (LL Cool J).

Aside from that, on whom was the movie In Too Deep based?

Information that is relevant. This film is based on a novel about the assassination of a Boston gangster called Darryl Whiting, who was really born Duane O. Gittings, thus the moniker GOD.

What does the phrase “In Too Deep” mean?

“Being in over one’s head” refers to being entangled in a tough circumstance from which one cannot escape. This phrase often refers to being so deeply engrossed in something that you are unable to extract yourself and have no idea how to get out of it. “In too deep” is also the title of a song.
