Locations Revealed For WWE Backlash &amp; Extreme Rules Including Touring Schedule

WWE has to book their schedule well in advance to get things planned out. They have a lot of moving pieces going at all times, after all. Now it looks like June and July for 2019 is locked down.

Rajah reports that WWE’s June and July schedule is all worked out and this means that two pay-per-view events now have date and location. It is interesting how WWE neglected to book an all-female pay-per-view this time around, of course, that can always change as well.

WWE will be holding their Backlash event on June 16th in San Diego California this year. The following month will see WWE Extreme Rules take place in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania which is a city that knows a thing or two about hardcore wrestling.
