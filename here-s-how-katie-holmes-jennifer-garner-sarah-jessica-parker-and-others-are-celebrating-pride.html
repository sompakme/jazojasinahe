Here's how Katie Holmes, Jennifer Garner, Sarah Jessica Parker, and others are celebrating pride

Some of the best "happy pride" moments included

    It's pride month, a time for the celebration of LGBTQ+ communities around the world, where we honor and remember their struggles, and cheer them on as they take on the world and spread love.

    MORE: 10 binge-worthy LGBTQ+ shows you need to watch

    While pride month is starting to wind down, some of the world's most famous allies and celebrities are just picking up steam. Several of them are sharing their own social media dedications to pride and commemorating the occasion in their own way.

    WATCH: Jennifer Garner shows off her impressive hidden talent

    Jennifer Garner

    The actress just kept it simple and beautiful, sharing a video made by photographer and filmmaker Jaimen Hudson. The stunning clip showed dolphins emerging out of the water, flipping through the rainbow being created by the dew, with Coldplay's playing in the background.

    View post on Instagram 

    Jennifer Garner's post for Pride celebrated the colors of the rainbow

    Sarah Jessica Parker

    The star was able to pay homage to both Pride and New York City with her post, where she included a few pictures from New York's famous hot dog chain, Grays Papaya, the current book she's reading ( by Nadia Owusu), and a scene of New Yorkers celebrating Pride. "I couldn’t love this city more. X,SJ" she wrote.

    View post on Instagram 

    Sarah Jessica Parker paid tribute to Pride and New York City

    Katie Holmes

    Katie was another who celebrated Pride in NYC, showing off the way her fellow New Yorkers were celebrating around the Washington Square Park area. Her post featured rainbow socks with Nikes, face paint, people adorned in the pride, trans, and bi colors and flags, and using chalk to write messages on the ground, an intimate look into New York's unique culture.

    View post on Instagram 

    Katie Holmes provided an intimate look into NYC celebrations

    Priyanka Chopra

    Speaking of Pride in NYC, and keeping our theme, Priyanka decided to keep things more personal and shared her own look for the day, wearing a gorgeous sheer white combo with stunning gold bling. "Sunday’s OOTD. Feeling the love in NYC… Happy Pride!" she captioned it.

    View post on Instagram 

    Priyanka Chopra showed off her Pride OOTD

    Nicola Coughlan

    Nicola shared a graffiti mural of her character from , who came out as a lesbian at the end of the show’s first season. "How cool is this!? Thank you @emmaleneblake for this epic mural of Wee Clare and @tinydavyq for sending it to me #HappyPride Angels" she captioned it.

    View post on Instagram 

    Nicola Coughlan gave a shout-out to her character

    Julianne Moore

    The actress took a different route, sharing a throwback of a shoot she'd done with British fashion photographer, Tim Walker. "Happy #pride #loveislove," she wrote for the photograph which shows her off in a long blue gown (a snuggie?) and coiffed hair, with a rainbow behind her and surrounded by sunflowers

    View post on Instagram 

    Julianne Moore used the opportunity to share a throwback

    Jessica Chastain

    And back to NYC we go, this time with actress Jessica Chastain, who showed off her love for pride with a little rainbow flag sticker on her chest. She uploaded a looped video soundtracked by Lady Gaga's LGBTQ+ classic , showing off the sticker as she basked in the sunlight, simply captioning it with a bunch of colorful hearts.

    View post on Instagram 

    Jessica Chastain got a little rainbow sticker to mark the occasion
