First-Time-Ever Bout Confirmed For 2/14 AEW Dynamite

AEW has a lot of things going for it right now as the company finally has momentum again thanks to a series of consistently good shows and solid matches. Ahead of Dynamite this week, Tony Khan confirmed a huge first-time-ever match for Dynamite.

This week’s episode of AEW Dynamite will take place at the HEB Center in Austin, Texas and feature tons of great wrestling. It will also deal with the fallout of AEW Collision last week.

The Blackpool Combat Club and FTR engaged in a brawl last week on AEW Collision. Therefore, AEW President Tony Khan took to Twitter and announced that Dax Harwood would be squaring off against Jon Moxley in a singles match on Dynamite this week.

‘‘TOMORROW, Valentine’s Day

@HEBCenterAustin, TX
Wednesday Night #AEWDynamite
On TBS, 8pm ET/7pm CT

@DaxFTR vs @JonMoxley

After Saturday’s brawl, there’s no love lost between FTR/BCC!
Now, for the first time ever 1-on-1:
DAX vs MOX
Live on
@TBSNetwork
Valentine’s Day TOMORROW!”

AEW Dynamite Lineup (2/14/2024):

  • Number one contender’s match for the TNT Championship: Daniel Garcia vs. Adam Copeland
  • Jon Moxley vs. Dax Harwood
  • Toni Storm debuts new film
  • Young Bucks vs. Top Flight
  • Skye Blue vs. Willow Nightingale
  • We’ll hear from Hangman Adam Page, Samoa Joe, and Swerve Strickland
