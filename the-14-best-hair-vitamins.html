The 14 Best Hair Vitamins

If you've ever dealt with dry, brittle, or broken hair, then you know how frustrating it can be to sift through every deep conditioner and cream on the market, hoping to find one that will finally help. But what if the problem isn't actually on the surface of your hair?

According to Lisa Moskovitz, RD, CDN, and CEO of New York Nutrition Group, hair loss, and breakage can be caused by a lack of vitamins and minerals. She elaborates: "Key nutrients to highlight for hair health include iron, zinc, selenium, protein (amino acids), anti-inflammatory fats like olive oil and avocado, and antioxidants." Your hair may suffer if you're not getting enough of these nutrients in your diet. That's where supplements come in — to fill in the gaps and restore hair health.

We set out to find the best hair vitamins available. In our research, we reviewed customer feedback as well as looked at key factors like ingredient quality and, of course, effectiveness. For those looking for an organic option, our top pick is Garden of Life's Vegan Collagen Builder, which has earned several certifications and improves skin and nail health and hair quality.

It's important to note that you should see a nutritionist or doctor before taking any supplements. They can help you pinpoint your deficiencies and select the proper vitamin.
