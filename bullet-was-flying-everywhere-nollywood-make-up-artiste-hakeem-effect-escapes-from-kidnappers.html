Bullet Was Flying Everywhere: Nollywood Make-Up Artiste Hakeem Effect Escapes From Kidnappers

  • Hakeem Effect has made a clip of what suspected kidnappers did to his bus while he was travelling with his staff
  • He stated that gunmen attacked people on the road around Ikenne, adding that some were killed while others were taken away
  • He said his driver was shot but noted that they were able to escape, although bullets damaged the bus they travelled in

Nollywood special effect make-up artist Hakeem Ajibola, better known as Hakeem Effect, has every reason to be thankful to his creator for keeping him alive.

He shared a recording of an attack on his life and that of his staff while travelling through Ikenne in Ogun State.

Gunmen attacked the award-winning beauty specialist, and bullets badly damaged the bus he was travelling with.

Hakeem Effect says his driver was shot

In the clip, Hakeem Effect, who was responsible for Femi Adebayo's scary face in Jagun Jagun, noted that his driver tried a lot to save all the people on the bus.

Hakeem said he gave the driver the go-ahead to manoeuvre it even if it meant the bus would be badly damaged.

The make-up artist revealed that his driver, known as Legba, was shot at a close range, and bullets were flying everywhere during the attack.

Hakeem Effect thanked God for his life and added that his wife and all the people who were on the bus with him came out alive.
