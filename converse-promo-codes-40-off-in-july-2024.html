FAQs

Does Converse offer free shipping?

Yes, Converse offers free shipping on all orders over $75, or for logged-in Converse.com users. For orders under $75, standard shipping typically costs $8 and takes 3-5 working days. You can also opt for 2-day shipping for $8, or next-day shipping for $20. 

Does Converse have an email newsletter?

Yes, customers who sign up for the Converse email newsletter will receive 15% off their next order. Simply enter your email address into the pop-up box at the footer of the website to receive your exclusive Converse promo code. Not only will you be making some excellent savings, but you’ll also be the first to hear about exclusive sale events, product launches, other ways to save, and more. 

Does Converse offer a student discount?

Yes, if you’re a student, Converse offers a 10% student discount when you confirm your student status using UniDays. You will then receive an exclusive Converse coupon code to copy and paste into the promo code box at the checkout. 

Can I shop the Converse sale?

Yes, simply head to the ‘Sale’ page on the Converse website to find savings all year round. In the past, customers have saved up to 40% on stylish footwear and accessories. Converse also runs sale events during seasonal periods such as Black Friday, Cyber Monday, Christmas, New Year’s, and Labor Day. 

What is the returns policy at Converse?

At Converse, they have a 30-day returns policy from the date of receipt. During seasonal periods such as Christmas and New Year, this period is extended to 60 days. You must return your item in its original condition, with all tags and accessories attached.
