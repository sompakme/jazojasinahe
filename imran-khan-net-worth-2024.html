Early Life and Cricket Career

Imran Khan’s journey to wealth began with his cricket career. Born into an affluent family, he had the privilege of quality education and access to cricket from a young age. His talent quickly became apparent, and he went on to lead Pakistan to its first and only Cricket World Cup victory in 1992. The fame and success in cricket not only brought him wealth but also endorsements and opportunities to work as a cricket commentator.

Transition into Politics

After retiring from cricket, Imran Khan founded the Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) in 1996. His political career, although slow to start, eventually gained momentum, leading to his election as the Prime Minister of Pakistan in 2018. The salary and perks associated with this high office contributed to his net worth, albeit a small fraction compared to his cricket earnings.

Income from Autobiographies and Publications

Imran Khan is also an author, having written several autobiographies and books on cricket and politics. The royalties and advances from these publications have added to his wealth over the years.
