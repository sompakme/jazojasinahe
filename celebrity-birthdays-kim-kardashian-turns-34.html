Celebrity birthdays: Kim Kardashian turns 34

    Happy birthday, Kim Kardashian! As the reality TV star, fashion designer and socialite celebrates turning 34, HELLO! Online takes a look at what the stars have in store for Kim, and for you...

    Click here to read your horoscope.

    Kim Kardashian is one of four children born to Robert Kardashian, OJ Simpson's successful trial lawyer, who died in 2003, and his wife Kris and is one of the biggest stars on the planet.  

    Her dominance began with a series that showcased the clan's fabulous LA lifestyle.

    The reality TV queen has built up her empire with DASH, a chain of boutiques she manages with sisters Khloe and Kourtney, selling women's clothing. Added to that are endorsement deals which have included cup cake mix, trainers, diet products and, even at one point, a debit card (').

    She has one of the world's most influential media presences with over 24 million followers hanging on her every word - more tan Beyoncé or even the Pope.

    Intense interest also surrounds her love life.

    The mother-of-one has also been married twice – the first time was for four years in her twenties; the second for 72 days to basketball player Kris Humphries.

    The reality TV star began dating rapper Kanye West in Spring 2012. The pair had been friends for many years before dating, and she gave birth to their daughter North - nicknamed Nori - on 15 June 2013. The pair got engaged just months later, and in May 2014 they celebrated their marriage with lavish wedding celebrations in Paris, France, and Florence, Italy.
