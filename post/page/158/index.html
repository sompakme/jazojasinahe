What does the red light mean on my HoMedics humidifier?

published on August 30, 2024
Answer The presence of a red light indicates that you are short on water. More water should be added. The colour green indicates that you have enough water to generate mist. Even if you have thoroughly cleaned the unit and have plenty of water available, the red light will illuminate. People often inquire as to why their humidifier’s light is illuminated in red.
