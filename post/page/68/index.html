Are dogs allowed at Mt Rushmore?

published on September 17, 2024
Yes, dogs are allowed at Mt Rushmore National Memorial. However, there are certain rules and regulations that visitors must adhere to when bringing their furry friends to the memorial. Dogs are welcome in outdoor areas and on designated trails, but they must be on a leash at all times. It is also important for dog owners to clean up after their pets and dispose of waste properly.
