Is 400k a year middle class?

published on September 16, 2024
If we define the middle class strictly by the median income, $400,000 is certainly not middle class. The median income in the United States was approximately $69,000 per year in 2019, a fraction of $400,000. The median income is roughly the same or lower in most major U.S. cities. What is a middle class salary in 2020? Household incomes have risen considerably since 1970, but those of middle-class households have not climbed nearly as much as those of upper-income households.
