Candy Paint &amp; Gold Teeth

Check out Candy Paint & Gold Teeth , the latest track from Waka Flocka, featuring Bun B & Ludacris which dropped on Thursday, June 7th, 2012. Waka Flocka's movement grows stronger with each new release, and Candy Paint & Gold Teeth has further strengthened that fact. It's a solid addition to the commendable body of work Waka Flocka has been creating since stepping foot in the rap game, and we're definitely looking forward to what Waka Flocka has on deck.

Check out Waka Flocka on Fist Pump , featuring B.o.B.. Check out Waka Flocka's profile page for the most recent info, news, songs, and mixtapes. Follow Waka Flocka on Facebook and Twitter, if you aren't already.
