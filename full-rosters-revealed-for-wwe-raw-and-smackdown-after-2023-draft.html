Full Rosters Revealed for WWE RAW and SmackDown After 2023 Draft

Following the 2023 WWE Draft, the updated rosters have been revealed for both WWE Raw and SmackDown. Here is a detailed breakdown of the superstars on each roster:

SmackDown Men’s Roster:

The top star of the blue brand is none other than the current Undisputed WWE Universal Champion, Roman Reigns.

Other prominent names on the roster include Edge, AJ Styles, Bobby Lashley, Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso, Solo Sikoa, and Rey Mysterio. The United States Champion, Austin Theory, is also a part of the SmackDown roster, alongside Sheamus, LA Knight, Ridge Holland, Butch, Karrion Kross, Montez Ford, Cameron Grimes, Santos Escobar, Joaquin Wilde, Cruz Del Toro, Angelo Dawkins, Luke Gallows, Karl Anderson, Ashante Thee Adonis, Top Dolla, Elton Prince, Kit Wilson, and Grayson Waller.

RAW Women’s Roster:

The RAW women’s division is headlined by the reigning SmackDown Women’s Champion, Rhea Ripley.

Other big names on the roster include Becky Lynch, Trish Stratus, Ronda Rousey, Shayna Baszler, WWE Women’s Tag Team Champions Liv Morgan and Raquel Rodriguez, NXT Women’s Champion Indi Hartwell, Natalya, Candice LeRae, Chelsea Green, Sonya Deville, Zoey Stark, Valhalla, Katana Chance, Kayden Carter, Maxxine Dupri, Nikki Cross, Dana Brooke, Piper Niven, Xia Li, Tegan Nox, Emma, and Free Agents.

Raw Men’s Roster:

The Raw men’s division is led by Cody Rhodes, Seth Rollins, and Drew McIntyre. The Undisputed WWE Tag Team Champions, Kevin Owens and Sami Zayn, are also a part of the Raw roster.

Other big names include Shinsuke Nakamura, Matt Riddle, Bronson Reed, Ricochet, Braun Strowman, Finn Balor, Damian Priest, Dominik Mysterio, Xavier Woods, Kofi Kingston, Chad Gable, Otis, The Miz, Intercontinental Champion Gunther, Giovanni Vinci, Ludwig Kaiser, Mansoor, Mace, Erik, Ivar, Apollo Crews, Dexter Lumis, JD McDonagh, Odyssey Jones, Jinder Mahal, Veer Mahaan, Sanga, Johnny Gargano, Angel Garza, Humberto Carrillo, Akira Tozawa, Riddick Moss, and Free Agents.

SmackDown Women’s Roster:

The SmackDown women’s division is led by the reigning RAW Women’s Champion, Bianca Belair.

Other notable names on the roster include Charlotte Flair, Bayley, Asuka, Dakota Kai, IYO SKY, Zelina Vega, Michin, NXT Women’s Tag Team Champions Alba Fyre and Isla Dawn, Shotzi, Lacey Evans, Scarlett, B-Fab, Tamina, and Free Agents.

Overall, the updated rosters have a mix of established and rising stars, with each brand having its own unique flavor. Fans can expect to see some exciting matchups and storylines in the coming weeks and months, as WWE continues to put on some of the best wrestling entertainment in the world.
