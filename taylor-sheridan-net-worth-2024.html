Early Life and Career Beginnings

Taylor Sheridan was born in Cranfills Gap, Texas, and grew up with a deep appreciation for the American West, which would later influence his work. He began his career as an actor, with notable roles in television series such as “Veronica Mars” and “Sons of Anarchy.”
