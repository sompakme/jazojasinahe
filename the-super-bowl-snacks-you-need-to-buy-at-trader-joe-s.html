The Super Bowl Snacks You Need to Buy at Trader Joe's

The big game is almost here, and that can only mean one thing — the countdown to Super Bowl snacking can commence! Oh, yeah, sure, there will be football, too. But if we’re really being honest with ourselves, we look forward to the foodie aspect of Super Bowl Sunday just as much as we enjoy the action on the field…if not more.

Related story Trader Joe's New Summer Beverage Is Giving Total 'Bridgerton' Vibes & Is Perfect for Cocktails

Of course, Super Bowl-level snacking means you (a) either step up your game or (b) you resign yourself to an evening filled with greasy chips, bar nuts and, well, whatever other generic junk food you have stashed in your pantry. You can do better than that though, even if you don’t have the energy to make an assortment of unique chicken wing recipes this year, because Trader Joe’s has your back.

Not only can you finger perfectly portable and convenient finger foods, but it won’t set you back your entire paycheck just to feed your friends. Plus, since TJ’s food is artisanal and interesting, you’ll come off looking like the MVP host who put extra time and energy into creating a fun Super Bowl menu for everyone.

You can score (sorry, couldn’t resist) the following quick, delicious and Super Bowl worthy snacks at your local Trader Joe’s just in time for the big game.

A version of this story was originally published January 2020. 

  • Creamy Spinach & Artichoke Dip

    Is there any party appetizer more iconic than spinach and artichoke dip? Serve it with pita wedges, tortilla chips, crackers, and other dippers.

  • Hardwood Smoked Pulled Pork

    Toss this smoked pulled pork with a little extra barbecue sauce, place it on slider rolls with coleslaw, and you’ve got a great Super Bowl party snack. You can also use it to make nachos or flautas!

  • Jalapeño & Cream Cheese Crispy Wontons

    Can’t decide between jalapeño poppers and crab rangoons? No worries! Trader Joe’s basically combined the two with their jalapeño and cream cheese crispy wontons. Serve with sweet chili sauce for dipping.

  • Pizza Party Potato Chips

    Chips and dip are a Super Bowl necessity, but you can take things to the next level with these brand new Pizza Party Potato Chips from Trader Joe’s.

  • Parmesan Pastry Pups

    Trader Joe’s Parmesan Pastry Pups are staple for a reason. Everyone loves pigs in a blanket, and this version is just elevated enough to work for game night or a cocktail party.

  • Pita Bite Crackers

    Whether you’re serving a cheesy baked dip, hummus and Boursin, or a full cheese platter at your Super Bowl party, you’ll need crackers to go along with the spread. We love these crispy, puffy, lightly salted pita bread crackers.

  • Chicken Meatballs

    Sweet and sour or BBQ slow cooker meatballs are a classic part of any Super Bowl party. But you can make the recipe a little easier — and slightly healthier — by using these Trader Joe’s fully cooked chicken meatballs as the base.

  • Papadums Lentil and Chickpea Crisps

    This crunchy, crispy lentil and chickpea papadum crackers are like a smaller version of restaurant papadums. Serve them with mango chutney or any of your favorite dips.

  • Lightly Salted Edamame

    Let’s be real: You’re going to eat some not-so-healthy stuff on Super Bowl Sunday. It’s probably best to balance it out with a few lighter snacks, like this salted edamame. Hand-picked in Southeast Asia and flash-frozen, it’s delightfully snappy once thawed. 

  • Sweet & Salty Umami Crunchies Rice Crackers

    If you’ve ever had Trader Joe’s Crispy Crunchy Mochi Rice Nuggets, you know how delicious they are. But these extra-crispy and crunchy rice crackers, flavored with a sweet and salty glaze, might be even tastier. Pick up a few bags for your party — they’re even made gluten-free, thanks to using a rice-based, rather than wheat-based, soy sauce.

  • Chili Onion Crunch Sauce

    Are you up for a fun game of your own? See who can bring the best snack to go with this one-of-a -kind dipping sauce. 

  • Oven-Baked Cheese Bites With Black Summer Truffle

    Whether you just really love cheese (and, like, who doesn’t?) or you’ve got Super Bowl comany eating keto, these oven-baked cheese bites are a no-brainer. And they’re only $2.99 a bag, so you can stock up. 

  • Rolled Corn Tortilla Chips: Chili & Lime
