Jennifer Love Hewitt - Latest News &amp; Photos

Jennifer Love Hewitt found fame in the 1990s alongside Scott Wolf and Neve Campbell in Party of Five, and later cult movie classics I Know What You Did Last Summer (1997) and its 1998 sequel and the teen comedy film Can't Hardly Wait (1998). She returned to TV in 2005 with the hit Ghost Whisperer, and later worked on acclaimed series The Client List and  Criminal Minds. In 2018 she joined Fox drama 9-1-1 in its second season as Maddie Buckley, a dispatch operator and the sister of first responder Evan Buckley (played by Oliver Stark). Jennifer is married to Brian Hallisay and they have three children.
