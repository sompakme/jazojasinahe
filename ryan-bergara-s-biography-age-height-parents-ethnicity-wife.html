Buzzfeed Unsolved was launched in 2016. It first appeared on the BuzzFeed Blue YouTube channels currently known as BuzzFeed Multiplayer before being given its own YouTube channel, BuzzFeed Unsolved Network.

The show reveals unsolved criminal events and mysterious supernatural cases, including investigating haunted locations and demonic possessions.
