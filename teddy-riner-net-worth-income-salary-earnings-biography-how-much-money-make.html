Teddy Pierre-Marie Riner (French pronunciation: [t?.di/te.di?i.n? ]; born 7 April 1989) is a French judoka who competes in the World Judo Championships. He has won six gold medals at the World Championships, making him the first male judoka to accomplish so, as well as an Olympic gold medal. In addition, he has four gold medals from the European Championships to his credit. Levallois Sporting Club, located in the French town of Levallois-Perret, is where he spends his spare time
